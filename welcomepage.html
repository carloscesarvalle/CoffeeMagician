Welcome to Coffee Magician!

Little Valerie is a full-stack web developer student at IronHack Miami -but she leaves in LA! The sleepy ghosts are so hard to beat at 5am in the morning.

Luckily, the Coffee Magician can help her.

Touch every sleepy ghosts to turn them into coffee cups. If a coffee cup touches another sleepy ghost, you'll get double points.

It takes 100 points to wake up little Valerie.
